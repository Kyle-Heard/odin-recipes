Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Pasta
  • 1 Can of Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut Onions & Peppers
  2. Sautee Onions & Peppers
  3. Add Pasta Sauce and Simmer
  4. Boil Water
  5. Cook Pasta
  6. Drain Pasta
  7. Mix & Enjoy!

Serves 6
